Welcome to Laundry Wallah / Laundry Mart

"Laundry Mart" can refer to several businesses, including a specific dry cleaning service in Chennai that offers pickup, delivery, and other services, an online laundry service based in India called TheLaundryMart that uses technology for efficient delivery, or a general type of business that provides laundry services. It's important to distinguish between these based on context, as the original term could also refer to a self-service laundromat.

A laundromat is a self-service laundry facility where customers use coin-operated or card-operated machines to wash and dry their clothes. These businesses provide a convenient solution for those who don't own a washing machine, need to wash bulky items like blankets, or require a quick way to get a large load done at once. Beyond the self-service option, many laundromats also offer additional amenities like vending machines, folding tables, and seating, and some may provide full-service options such as wash-and-fold or dry cleaning.

Our Services

  • Laundry services: Washing, drying, and folding of garments. This is a core service, sometimes called "wash and fold".
  • Dry cleaning: A chemical-based cleaning process for delicate fabrics that can't be washed with water, such as woolens and designer wear.
  • Steam ironing: Professional steam ironing to give clothes a crisp, freshly pressed look.
  • Stain treatment: Pre-treating stains before the main washing process to ensure they are removed effectively.
  • Coin-operated facilities: Some locations provide self-service coin-operated washers and dryers for customers who want to do their own laundry.
